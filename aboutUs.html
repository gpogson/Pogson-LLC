About Us

Gavin Pogson is a dynamic and innovative Computer Science student at the University of Arizona, with a minor in Economics. He excels in programming languages like Java, Python, C, and JavaScript, and has a strong background in SQL, Git, and web development tools. Gavin's leadership skills are evident through his role as a Coordinator at the university's Computer Science Tutoring Center and as a founding member of Sigma Nu fraternity. His experience includes enhancing the online presence of Pogson Physical Therapy through website development. Passionate about economics and the stock market, Gavin enjoys applying his technical skills to areas like machine learning and database design. In his free time, he loves surfing, cooking, reading, and sports like tennis and golf.
